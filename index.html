What is Narrativistics?

Narrativistics is the theory that our reality, referred to as a "narrative", is one of an infinite number of narratives that exist. These narratives are "stacked on top of each other";

"There will be a layer between two layers, always have been, even if they appear identical, because the "layers" ... are a visualization guide to help our feeble human minds parse a more abstract phenomenon." From "Narrativistics and You", a seminar by J. Anslem Harkness
Narrativistics states that everything is fictional. Everything is controlled by the Elementary Narrative Particles. Each particle is explained later on in this website.
