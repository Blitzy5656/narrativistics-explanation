What is Narrativistics?

Narrativistics is the theory that our reality, referred to as a "narrative", is one of an infinite number of narratives that exist. These narratives are "stacked on top of each other", like how a sandwich's ingrediens are stacked on top if each other; only the sandwich is infinitly tall and there is no sandwich. Narrativistics states that everything is fictional. Everything is controlled by the Elementary Narrative Particles. Each particle is explained in this website.
